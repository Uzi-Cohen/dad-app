# Fashion Design Studio - Simple Instructions

## How to Start the App

### Windows:
1. Double-click the file called `start.bat`
2. Wait for it to finish loading (first time takes 2-3 minutes to download everything)
3. Open Google Chrome or your web browser
4. Go to: **http://localhost:3000**

### Mac/Linux:
1. Double-click the file called `start.sh`
2. Wait for it to finish loading (first time takes 2-3 minutes to download everything)
3. Open Google Chrome or your web browser
4. Go to: **http://localhost:3000**

**Note:** The very first time you run it, it will automatically set up everything for you. After that, it starts in 10-15 seconds!

## How to Use the App

### Making New Dress Photos

1. Click **"Generate Images"** at the top
2. **Upload a photo** of your dress (optional but recommended)
3. **Type what you want** in the text box, like:
   - "Change to royal blue color"
   - "Add lace sleeves"
   - "Make it floor-length"
   - "Add gold embroidery"
4. Click **"Generate Image"**
5. Wait 10-30 seconds
6. Click **"Download"** to save the image

### Making Videos of Dresses

1. Click **"Generate Videos"** at the top
2. **Upload a photo** of your dress
3. **Type the movement** you want, like:
   - "Dress flowing in the wind"
   - "Model walking on runway"
   - "Fabric moving gracefully"
4. Click **"Generate Video"**
5. Wait 1-3 minutes (videos take longer)
6. Click **"Download"** to save the video

### Viewing All Your Creations

1. Click **"Gallery"** at the top
2. See all your generated images and videos
3. Click any image or video to view it larger
4. Click **"Download"** to save them to your computer

## Important Notes

- **First startup takes 2-3 minutes** to download everything
- **After that, starts in 10-15 seconds**
- **Images take 10-30 seconds** to generate
- **Videos take 1-3 minutes** to generate
- **Internet connection required** - The AI runs in the cloud

## Stopping the App

1. Find the window that opened when you started the app
2. Press **Ctrl+C** (Windows/Linux) or **Command+C** (Mac)
3. Or just close the terminal window

## Need Help?

Everything is already set up for you! Just double-click the start file and open your browser.

If something doesn't work:
1. Make sure you have internet connection
2. Try closing and restarting the app
3. Contact your tech support person (the one who set this up for you)
